$$
p(D)e^{\alpha x}=p(\alpha)e^{\alpha x}
$$
Exponential Input theorem 
$$
y_p=\frac{e^{\alpha x}}{p(\alpha)}
$$
Assuming $$p(\alpha)\neq 0$$. What if $$p(\alpha)=0$$ ? We have exponential shift rule:
$$
p(D)e^{\alpha x}
$$
